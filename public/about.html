About Me

With over two years of experience managing diverse projects in the software development and financial services sectors, I bring a robust blend of technical expertise, leadership, and a passion for delivering results that exceed expectations.

In my current role as a Project Manager in a financial institution, I successfully led the implementation of a fraud detection solution to enhance real-time transaction security in the banking sector. Despite encountering challenges such as data incompleteness and failed API calls, I employed meticulous planning, stakeholder engagement, and innovative problem-solving to drive the project's progress.

Additionally, I coordinated the development of a UI/UX project monitoring portal, seamlessly integrating contributions from front-end and back-end teams to create an efficient tool for tracking project milestones.

I have honed my project management expertise across various methodologies, including both Agile and Waterfall approaches. This versatility allows me to tailor workflows to the unique needs of each project. For instance, I have used the structured phases of the Waterfall methodology to manage projects requiring detailed upfront planning and precise deliverable sequencing, ensuring milestones are met without compromising quality or scope. Key strengths include:

  • Strategic Planning and Execution: Crafting and managing detailed project plans to meet organizational goals within strict timelines and budgets.
  • Team Leadership: Fostering collaboration and maintaining accountability within diverse, cross-functional teams.
  • Risk and Change Management: Identifying risks and implementing proactive strategies to mitigate potential disruptions.
  • Stakeholder Communication: Effectively aligning technical teams and executives to ensure transparency and consensus.
  • Hybrid Methodologies: Leveraging both Agile and Waterfall methodologies to optimize project delivery based on specific project requirements.

Beyond my professional experiences, I am deeply passionate about leveraging technology and data to drive business innovation. My recent exploration into machine learning and artificial intelligence has further enhanced my analytical capabilities, equipping me to approach complex problems with fresh insights and strategies.
